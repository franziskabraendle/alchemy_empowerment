Create as many elements as you can!

  • This game is called "Tiny Pixels". It is your task to dicover elements.
  • You can create elements by proposing experiments. Experiments let you combine two elements.
  • You start out with 4 basic elements in your inventory. You can click on an element to add it to the current experiment. Once you are sure that you want to test a combination of two elements, you can click on "Let's find out".
  • If the experiment did not create a new element, then nothing happens. If the experiments created a new element, then this element gets added to your inventory and you can use it for future experiments.
  • It is your goal to create as many elements as possible. In principle, there are 520 elements you could discover. However, no one has ever discovered all of them.
  • You can play as long as you want (up to 5 hours) and we will pay you $0.10 for every element in your final inventory.
  • If you click on "I give up", the game ends, the number of elements in your inventory becomes your final score, and you will be directed to the next page.
